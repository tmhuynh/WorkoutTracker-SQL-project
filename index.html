What is Workout Tracker


Workout Tracker is designed to replace your paper workout journal. It is a simple tracker which keeps track of your workout.

What makes Workout Tracker special

Developed recently by group of computer science students who were disappointed by the selection of workout tracking

apps, it combines the best features found in other apps with a minimalistic approach and a clean, easy-to-use interface.

Give it a try. We know you'll love it! Also this is just the start if you like it, we will keep on improving it!
